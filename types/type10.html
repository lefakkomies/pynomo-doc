Type 10¶

Type 10 is nomograph that has one curved line. It has functional relationship:

\[F_1(u)+F_2(v)F_3(w)+F_4(w)=0.\]

Simple example¶

This simple example plots nomograph for equation:

\[u + v w + w = 0.\]
